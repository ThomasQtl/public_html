Welcome

Presentation

Welcome to my website. My name is Thomas Quentel, second year student at ISIMA - Clermont Auvergne INP.
You will find on this site my various projects as well as my resume and my LinkedIn account.

From 2015 to 2021 I was male and female artistic gymnastics coach (3-15 years old) in the club SL Constantia Neudorf where I was responsible for the 6-9 years old competition from 2016 to 2021.

During my senior year (2018-2019), I had the opportunity to do a civic service (in parallel with high school) in the field of sport and youth.
That same year, I started the BAFA (General Session and Practical Internship), I trained to become Level 1 Federal Animator in Bi-Qualifying Gymnastics, I passed my PSC1 as well as the driving license.

From 2019 to 2021, I completed a CPGE in PTSI / PT* in Strasbourg.

Today, I am student at ISIMA in Clermont-Ferrand where I am Communication Manager of the BDE and the ReZZo club (Club Gaming) as well as Secretary of the Club Pixel (Club for creating video games). I also have the role of VSS Manager within the BDE where I have to take care of the prevention of Sexual and Gender-based Violence.

Finally, I got involved in a project that is close to my heart where we accompany a young high school student with a disability once a week to help him on the academic side but also socially. This is the Handitutorat project managed by Bruno Fouletier.
