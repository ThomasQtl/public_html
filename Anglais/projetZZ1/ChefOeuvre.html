Masterpiece

PioupioubrbgbrbrbgbrPPFFPPFFPFPFFPFFFFFFFF

Our masterpiece is called "PioupioubrbgbrbrbgbrPPFFPPFFPFPFFPFFFFFFFF".
The objective is to defend our base, which is located on the left, from the enemy invasion !
To move your ship, use the Z keys to go up and S to go down.
You can fire missiles with a mouse click.
Regarding the more technical part; enemies move at a theta angle each cycle.
At each cycle, a new angle theta is calculated using a Markov chain.
A score is incremented each time you kill an enemy and it is possible to restart the game from an end screen.
