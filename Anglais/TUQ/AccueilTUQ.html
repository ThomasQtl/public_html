TUQ

Presentation

TUQ is a Tetris that I did with Tanvir Uddin during my final year S in ISN specialty (Computer Science and Digital Science).
It was made on Processing which is based on the Java language.
The project is spread over 6 months and resulted in a 20/20 at the baccalaureate on my side. Tanvir had a little less because he had more difficulty with the French language and was very busy with the French administration throughout his school year.
It was for me, in addition to a computer project, a human apprenticeship to work with a person who had just arrived in France (Tanvir comes from Sri Lanka).
